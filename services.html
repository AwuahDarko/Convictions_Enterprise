Our Services

The two main issues that may trigger the success or otherwise of a business (SME), irrespective of the industry within which the business operates, are managerial skills and adequate capitalization. That is why Convictions Enterprise has stepped in to provide the relevant support system for the success and sustenance of the various business entities in Ghana.

Due to the growing recognition of the important role small and medium enterprises (SMEs) play in the development of small and large economies, the critical intervention by Convictions Enterprise is long overdue.

Our highly distinguished Professionals will help you in the areas of accounting issues, tax concerns, managerial skills, capital acquisition, suitable accommodation, market comprehension, product pricing, cash flow anticipations, reaction to competitions, overgeneralizations, customer dependence, uncontrolled growth, customer relations, etc.

Management And Capitalization

Whether you’re starting a business or expanding one, sufficient ready capital is essential. It is not enough to simply have sufficient financing; knowledge and planning are required to manage it well. These qualities ensure that entrepreneurs avoid common mistakes like securing the wrong type of financing, miscalculating the amount required, or underestimating the cost of borrowing money. This is the type of need that Convictions is here to meet.

Accounting Issues

For you to operate to your optimum level you need to have the peace of mind to work without been charged for tax evasions, that’s why Convictions says let me help you out with this. Again Convictions will help you in the area of book keeping, for you to be able to monitor your cash flows.

Market Comprehension

Convictions will help you get answers to the following questions; Who are your customers? How are you going to reach them? How loyal are your potential customers to their current supplier? Do customers keep coming back or do they just purchase from you one time? Does it take a long time to close a sale or are your customers more driven by impulse buying?

Product Pricing

Pricing is one of the significant factors that determine the growth of the business. You must clearly define your pricing strategy. You can be the cheapest or you can be the best, but if you try to do both, you’ll fail.
